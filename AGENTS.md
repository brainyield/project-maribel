**How do I enroll my child at Eaton Academic?**
The enrollment process starts with a free 15-minute consultation call where we learn about your child and your family's needs. Most of our programs accept rolling enrollment, meaning your child can start any time there's availability â€” you don't have to wait for a new semester. The Microschool has specific enrollment windows (August and January) but may accept mid-year transfers if space is available.

**Is there a registration fee?**
Yes, there is a one-time registration fee that varies by program. For the Microschool, it's $250. For Learning Pods and Eaton Online, it's $75. The registration fee is non-refundable and covers administrative processing and materials setup.

Learning Pod pricing varies by location and grade level, typically ranging from $150-$195 per month per student. Contact us for current pricing for specific pods.
