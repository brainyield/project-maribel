**Eaton Academic Privacy Policy**

**Last Updated:** [DATE]

Eaton Academic ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website eatonacademic.com and the Instagram account @eatonacademic, including our automated messaging assistant ("Maribel"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with us through Instagram Direct Messages, Instagram comments, our website, or any of our digital services.

By sending us a message on Instagram or interacting with our automated assistant, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Through Instagram Direct Messages

When you send us a Direct Message on Instagram, we collect:

- **Message content**: The text of messages you send to us and our replies to you
- **Instagram user ID**: Your unique Instagram identifier, used to maintain your conversation history
- **Instagram username and profile name**: Used to personalize our responses
- **Timestamps**: When messages are sent and received

### Information You Voluntarily Share in Conversation

During our conversations, you may choose to share additional information such as:

- Your name or your child's name
- Your child's grade level or age
- Your location or neighborhood
- Your email address (e.g., when booking a consultation)
- Your phone number
- Your preferences regarding educational programs

We only collect this information when you voluntarily provide it in conversation.

### Information from Instagram Comments

When you comment on one of our Instagram posts (e.g., commenting a keyword like "PODS" to receive more information), we collect:

- The content of your comment
- Your Instagram user ID
- The post you commented on

This information is used solely to send you a single informational Direct Message related to the keyword you commented.

### Automatically Collected Information

We do not use cookies or tracking pixels within Instagram DMs. However, our systems automatically log:

- Message delivery status
- Timestamps of interactions
- Conversation metadata (e.g., whether a conversation was escalated to a human team member)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Customer service**: To answer your questions about Eaton Academic's programs, schedules, pricing, and enrollment
- **Conversation continuity**: To remember context from previous conversations so you don't have to repeat yourself
- **Lead qualification**: To understand your needs and recommend appropriate programs for your family
- **Appointment booking**: To schedule consultation calls via Calendly when you request one
- **Escalation**: To route complex inquiries to a human team member when our automated assistant cannot adequately help you
- **Service improvement**: To analyze aggregate conversation patterns and improve the quality of our responses
- **Comment-to-DM engagement**: To send you a single informational DM when you comment a keyword on one of our posts

We do **not** use your information for:

- Targeted advertising
- Selling or renting to third parties
- Automated decision-making that produces legal effects
- Any purpose unrelated to providing you with information about Eaton Academic's educational services

---

## 3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your data is stored in a secure, cloud-hosted database (Supabase) with the following protections:

- **Encryption at rest**: All stored data is encrypted
- **Encryption in transit**: All data transfers use HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Access controls**: Only authorized Eaton Academic team members can access conversation data
- **No local storage**: Conversation data is not stored on personal devices

Our automated assistant uses AI technology (Anthropic's Claude API) to process and respond to messages. Message content is sent to this AI service for processing but is not used by the AI provider to train their models. We also use OpenAI's embedding API to match your questions with relevant information from our knowledge base.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We share your data only with the following service providers, strictly for the purposes described in this policy:

| Service Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---|---|---|
| **Meta (Instagram)** | Message delivery | Message content, user IDs |
| **Anthropic (Claude API)** | AI-powered response generation | Message content, conversation context |
| **OpenAI** | Knowledge base search (embeddings) | Message text (converted to mathematical vectors) |
| **Supabase** | Secure data storage | All collected data |
| **Calendly** | Appointment scheduling | Name, email (only when you request a booking) |

All service providers are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your conversation data as follows:

- **Conversation messages**: Retained for up to **12 months** from the date of your last interaction, then automatically deleted
- **Lead profile information**: Retained for up to **24 months** from the date of your last interaction
- **Conversation summaries**: Retained for up to **24 months** to enable continuity if you return
- **Analytics data**: Aggregated, anonymized analytics are retained indefinitely

You may request earlier deletion at any time (see Section 7).

- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data (see Section 7)
- **Opt out**: Stop interacting with our automated assistant at any time by simply not messaging us, or by requesting to speak with a human team member
- **Withdraw consent**: You may withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by contacting us
